Life in Michigan

“It's a great place to laze around and swim and fish when you want to. And the best place in the world to do nothing. It is beautiful country … And nobody knows about it but us.” - Ernest Hemingway

Reasons why life in Michigan is awesome

1. Enjoying the seasons

Spring
March-May: Average temps range between High:70 F and Low:25 F.
Summer
June-August: Average temps range between High:75 F and Low:57 F.
Fall
September-November: Average temps range between High:71 F and Low:32 F.
Winter
December-February: Average temps range between High:28 F and Low:-20 F.

2. Lake Life

We are surrounded by 4 out of the 5 Great Lakes
...That doesn't even include the thousands of inland lakes...
So most of our summer is spent in the water!

  • Lake Michigan
  • Lake Superior
  • Lake Huron
  • Lake Erie
  • Lake Ontario - not surrounded by
